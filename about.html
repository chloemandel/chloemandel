About Me







I am a high school senior from Wilton, Connecticut. As I've thought about my plans for after I graduate high school, I've reflected on the several interesting and rewarding opportunities I've had in my life so far, from traveling and living in Africa and Europe, to learning about computer science and how to code through a program founded by supermodel, Karlie Kloss. Currently in my senior year at Wilton High School, I've kept myself busy in the realm of technology by making websites, researching brownfield regeneration and autonomous vehicles, and photoshopping floorplans for an architectural design firm. My love of web design inspired me to code my own website using the Cloud9 IDE. To learn more about my interests and my travels, feel free to explore the other pages on my site.
